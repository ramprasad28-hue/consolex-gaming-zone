Plan Rules

  • Every membership expires after {{ plans.0.duration_days|default:30 }} days.
  • Unused hours do not carry forward.
  • Hours are deducted after every gaming session.
  • Membership hours belong only to the registered member.
  • Controller sharing does not increase free hours.
  • Membership cannot be transferred to another account.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do unused hours carry forward?

No. Membership hours are valid only for the {{ plans.0.duration_days|default:30 }}-day plan period and do not roll over.

Can I share my membership hours?

Hours belong to the registered member only. Controller sharing does not add free hours, and memberships cannot be transferred.

What happens when my hours run out?

Once membership hours are exhausted, further sessions are charged at the standard weekday/weekend hourly rates.

Can I cancel or get a refund?

Plans are billed monthly. Contact the CONSOLEX team on WhatsApp for cancellation and refund queries.
