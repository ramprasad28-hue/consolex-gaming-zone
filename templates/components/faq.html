Everything you need to know before you play.

You can book online through our website in under 60 seconds — just select your date, time, number of players, and duration. You'll receive a confirmation via WhatsApp. Walk-ins are also welcome subject to availability.

The minimum booking duration is 1 hour. You can book in 30-minute increments after the first hour. Maximum single-session booking is 4 hours.

Memberships include pre-paid gaming hours valid for 30 days. Basic (10 hrs weekdays), Standard (25+5 weekend hrs), and Pro (45 total hrs, all days). Members also get priority booking and exclusive discounts.

Membership hours do not roll over to the next month. We recommend choosing a plan that matches your expected usage. You can upgrade your plan at any time.

We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), cash, and all major credit/debit cards. Online bookings can be paid via UPI or card. Memberships can also be purchased in-store.

Cancellations made 2+ hours before the session start time receive a full refund. Cancellations within 2 hours receive a 50% refund. No-shows are non-refundable. Membership fees are non-refundable after activation.

Players below 16 years old must be accompanied by a parent or guardian. Some games are age-rated and we follow PEGI/ESRB guidelines. ID may be requested for age-restricted titles.

Outside food and drinks are not permitted inside the gaming zone. We have snacks, beverages, and refreshments available at the lounge counter. No smoking or alcohol is allowed on the premises.
